La Barraca?

Que es esto? Making Mediterranean fresh tapas for you, that is our greatest passion. We've been inspired  mostly by the Spanish tapa.

You can visit us for a tapa to compliment your wine or a refreshing cocktail, but also for "La buena mesa" a richly filled table with good food. We make this happen in one of Maastricht's most beautiful and historic streets. Together with our Valencian chef and our international team, we are on a daily quest for the right balance between Mediterranean cuisine and our burgundian Maastricht.

Frequently asked questions

Just a question

We've tried to answer some of your questions.

Allergens

First of all, we have a fairly wide selection of vegetarian tapas in our restaurant. Allergies and dietary requirements can also be indicated in the online reservation. It is important, however, that in case of serious allergies, you look at the menu beforehand and of course indicate it to the waiters on this evening. We will do our best to serve our tapas in the most appropriate way.

How to eat at la Barraca?

We've made a selection of Mediterranean tapas on our menu, which you can enjoy after work, after shopping, or for dinner with friends, family or as a couple. The relaxed atmosphere that comes with eating tapas and drinking good wine makes for a cosy 'Mediterranean' experience together. Our approach is therefore, not that everyone has their own plate of food (main course), but they order and share different tapas so everyone can taste as much as possible. We try to realise a complete meal on the table with tapas (smaller dishes). Our tapas are sometimes larger than expected and are prepared to be shared.

How it works at La Barraca?

You are welcome  with up to 7 people. Up to this number we can prepare a table for you. You can easily book a table online. You can also change your reservation and if necessary cancel your table yourself. At La Barraca we work with shifts, you can choose these yourself and they will be clearly indicated in our confirmation email. We ask for your cooperation. If you are with several people or you want to dine a little longer, we advise you to choose the last shift. Of course we have to keep to the set closing time.
